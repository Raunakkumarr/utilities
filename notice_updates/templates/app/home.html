What is this?

Many IOE or other competitive aspirants works hard enough for the test but it is not possible to always keep checking the exam notices website daily for every single update.
So, what I did is I created a bot to scrap those websites for new notices and save those to an online database. Whenever there is a new notice posted on the site, the bot sends email automatically to the registered emails.
You just have to provide your email and select the website for which you want to get updates for and subscribe to our app.
